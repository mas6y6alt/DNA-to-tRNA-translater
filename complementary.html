Complementary DNA Base Pairs

DNA, or deoxyribonucleic acid, is the molecule found in all living cells that contains the genetic instructions for an organism's development, function, and reproduction.

Since DNA is a double helix structure it has two base pairs connected on every rong. These base pairs are complementary meaning that they are opposite of each other. For instance A is paired with T and G is paired with C.
